About Me

Hi there! 😁 Thank you for visiting my page!


I am a postgraduate student in control science and engineering, focusing on the development of controller on intelligent vehicles. I have a strong interest in machine learning, computer vision and deep learning, especially applying these methods to solve the perception and decision in intelligent vehicle systems. I am proficient at C, C++ and Python programming, modeling of complex system and design controller, familiar to building deep learning model with Pytorch. I have rich experience on developing automotive electronic control unit, including developing, deploying and testing.


I aim to become an excellent engineer, specialist in intelligent vehicles and autonomous driving. I've got my M.E. from Harbin Institute of Technology in June 2022. Now I am seeking a Ph.D. position. 🖐 If you have any interest in me, please feel free to contact!

Shanghong Li, Linhui Zhao.
